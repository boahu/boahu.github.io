Important notice

ParkRelay helps coordinate real-time availability, but it does not guarantee a parking spot. Street parking depends on traffic, timing, and local conditions.

  • No payments, no “spot selling”, no reservations.
  • Use at your own discretion and follow all traffic/parking rules.
  • We are not affiliated with the City of New York or any government agency.
How it works

1) Leaver

When you’re about to leave a street parking spot, you tap Leaving. The app uses your location to confirm you’re inside the NYC pilot boundary and to start the handoff timer.

2) Hunter

Nearby drivers who tap Hunting can be matched. The system selects one winner (to avoid chaos). Only the winner receives the precise spot location.

3) Relay

The winner gets guidance/ETA and time-window prompts. If the leaver cancels (e.g., returns home), the hunter is notified so nobody wastes time.

Permissions & Data Use (plain English)

Location (required for the pilot)

ParkRelay uses precise location to:

  • confirm you’re inside the NYC pilot boundary before starting Hunting/Leaving,
  • match leavers with nearby hunters,
  • compute ETA/routing and proximity confirmation during an active handoff.

If you deny location, the core pilot actions won’t work.

Notifications (recommended)

Notifications help with time-sensitive events:

  • winner assignment,
  • cancellations,
  • proximity / timing prompts during an active handoff.

You can use the app without notifications, but you may miss critical updates.
