Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-23

ParkRelay is a pilot app that helps drivers coordinate street parking handoffs. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Important summary

  • We use precise location to run the pilot (geo-gating, matching, proximity/confirmation).
  • We use account data (email and authentication) so you can sign in and participate.
  • We may use push notifications for time-sensitive pilot events (assignment/cancellation/proximity alerts).
  • We do not sell your personal data.

Information we collect

1) Account & authentication

  • Email address (when you sign up/sign in).
  • Authentication data (e.g., session tokens) needed to keep you signed in.

If you use email/password sign-in, your password is handled by our authentication provider (Supabase Auth). We do not store passwords in plaintext.

2) Location data (sensitive)

  • Precise location (GPS latitude/longitude) while you use pilot features (e.g., Hunting, Leaving, navigation/proximity screens).
  • Location accuracy information may be processed to improve reliability and reduce misuse (for example, ignoring very inaccurate fixes).

We use location to: (a) verify you are inside the selected pilot city boundary before starting core actions, (b) match leavers with nearby hunters, (c) compute routing/ETA, and (d) support proximity confirmation/alerts during an active handoff.

3) Notifications

  • Push notification token (a device/app identifier) to deliver notifications.
  • Notification events related to pilot activity (e.g., assignment, cancellation, proximity alerts).

4) Usage & diagnostics

  • Diagnostics such as timestamps, app version, and error logs to improve reliability and troubleshoot issues.

How we use your data

  • Provide the service: eligibility, geo-gating, matching, handoff flow, routing/ETA.
  • Safety & integrity: reduce abuse and improve reliability during active pilot sessions.
  • Notifications: send time-sensitive pilot messages if you enable notifications.
  • Support: respond if you contact us.

Sharing & service providers

We share data only with service providers required to operate the app:

  • Supabase (authentication, database, and server functions that power the pilot backend).
  • OpenRouteService (routing/ETA). Routing requests are made to provide directions/ETA; the API key is stored server-side (not in the client app).

We do not sell your personal data.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed to operate the pilot, comply with legal obligations, and troubleshoot issues. Retention can vary by data type (for example, active-session data vs. diagnostics).

Your choices

  • You can decline permissions (location/notifications), but core pilot features may not work without them.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data (see “Contact”).

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data, including encryption in transit where supported. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children

ParkRelay is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

Email: parkrelayteam@gmail.com

For deletion requests, email us from the address used to sign in and include the subject: “ParkRelay deletion request”.

Changes to this policy

If we change this Privacy Policy, we will update the “Last updated” date on this page.
